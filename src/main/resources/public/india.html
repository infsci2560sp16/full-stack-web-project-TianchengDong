India

Delhi

Delhi is Mystery, magic, mayhem. India’s capital is littered with the relics of lost empires. A recommended way to glimpse is to take one of the city’s tours from a former street child’s view of Old Delhi to a cycle tour along the Yamuna.

Mumbai(Bombay)

Mumbai is full of dreamers and hard-labourers, stray dogs and exotic birds, artists and servants and fisherfolk and crorepatis and lots and lots of people. It has India’s most prolific film industry, and some of Asia’s biggest slums.

Jaipur

Jaipur, Rajasthan’s capital, is an enthralling historical city and the gateway to India’s most flamboyant state. It's colourful, chaotic streets ebb and flow with a heady brew of old and new. The grandeur of Amber Fort is Jaipur’s star attraction.
